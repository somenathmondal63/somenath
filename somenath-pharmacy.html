🏥 What We Offer

Our Healthcare Services

Complete diagnostic and pharmaceutical care available at Somenath Pharmacy.

💊

Medicines & Pharma

Wide range of prescription and OTC medicines, generic drugs, vitamins, supplements, and healthcare products — all sourced from certified manufacturers.

🩻

X-Ray Diagnostics

Digital X-Ray services for chest, bone, joints, and more. Quick results, minimal wait time, and expert radiologist interpretation available.

🩸

Blood Tests

Comprehensive blood testing including CBC, sugar, lipid profile, thyroid, kidney function, liver function tests, and many more at affordable rates.

🩺

Doctor Consultation

Visiting specialist doctors available on scheduled days. Get expert medical advice without the hassle of travelling to distant hospitals.

🏠

Home Sample Collection

Can't visit? Our trained staff will collect blood or other samples from your home at a convenient time. Call to schedule.

📋

Health Reports

Fast delivery of diagnostic reports. Digital copies sent via WhatsApp/email. Hard copies available for collection at the pharmacy.
